# MOBIUS Loans by Campus

## Report details

This report lists open loans of our materials checked out to MOBIUS libraries.

Reporter can enter a due date to ignore open loans later than that date as well as campus prefix used in locations (MST, MU, UMKC, UMSL).

Output includes: User barcode, last name (should be DcbSystem), loan status (should be open), loan date, due date, MOBIUS service pooint, item barcode, title, location, call number, volume, material type, loan type, loan UUID, item UUID.
